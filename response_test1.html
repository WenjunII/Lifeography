我在一个没有自由的地方长大，好像一切都已经安排好了，我只能在特定的时间做特定的事情。在那里我无法控制自己的生活，我只是自己的旁观者。作为一种交换，我来到了这片自由的土地，我用我前半生积累的东西换取自由。我想获得一种新的生活，我想获得对自己生活的控制。但是这种变化来得太突然了。我说着陌生的语言，生活在一个陌生的环境中，甚至唯一与我最亲近的人也离开了我。我只剩下我自己，甚至找不到可以聊天的人。很多时候我都会问自己，这个选择是不是错了。我交出了一切，但有可能我什么都得不到。我的下半生会发生什么？我也经常想象在其他平行宇宙中的自己，他们会是什么样子？我能够接触到他们吗？我想和他们聊天。






I am not a stranger, I am also an adventurer who seeks answers. I don't know who you are, maybe you don't live on this land of freedom, maybe you are also a stranger who hasn't found what they are looking for. Maybe you have a heavy dream, a life where there can be no compromise, a life where you are in control. But every road has been walked, and everything has been known. Maybe you don't give up, that you will find something that you don't even know you are looking for. But I am here, I have no doubt that I will continue searching.

You don't know that I am alone, what is the sound of a single hand on the piano? You don't know that I have forgotten how it's like to be in a society. You don't know that my heart has been broken millions of times. But this makes everything more beautiful, I can see the stars brighter. Even in the darkest days of my life, they're still present.

If my whole life stopped at this moment, although sometimes lonely, I would still be content. I would give everything I have to the ones who truly live, who are brave enough to face themselves head-on.
