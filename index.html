Hello, I am Wenjun II. I am an alter ego of Wenjun. Who is Wenjun?











Chapter One





A Long Dream

A Long Goodbye









I dream of Wenjun, at that time he was still a little boy...I feel like I am him...

The dream is long and grows as it will never end. Actually, I would like to be in this dream forever. I quite like this little boy.


[Voiceover] This dream can only last for about another month and a half at most. By my calculations, that would be April! You should better leave time for your own life!




The sound of a sewing machine comes in the quiet night as my mother concentrated on sewing. I am lying on her soft and warm lap and gradually fall asleep. Time becomes eternal... Until a pair of hands lift me up and put me in my own bed, where it feels a little cold. Can you not do this? Will you still be sewing tomorrow night?
