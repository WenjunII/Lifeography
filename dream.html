离开了熟悉的环境，我进入了为高考作准备的高中新生活。在这里我似乎一直很难找到归属感，还是我已经厌倦了为学习成绩而活的生活？说不清楚。我渐渐喜欢上了生物实验室，并参与进那里的课外活动小组，培育厌氧细菌去清洁珠江。有些时候我会觉得，我也挺像实验室的器皿里面的细菌，被培育去完成任务。如果细菌有自我意识，它会怎样选择？


I leave the familiar environment and enter a new life in high school in preparation for the National College Entrance Examination. I always seem to have a hard time finding a sense of belonging there, or am I just tired of living for academic achievement? I can't say. I grow to be a fan of the biology lab and get involved in an extracurricular group there, growing anaerobic bacteria to clean the Pearl River. Sometimes I feel that I am quite like the bacteria in the vessel in the lab, which is being grown to accomplish a task. If bacteria has self-awareness, how would it choose?

Voice of Wenjun

毕业后经历了找工作和失业的恐惧后，生活终于开始安稳下来了，我也逐渐开始接受和习惯如其他人一样过着按部就班的日子。我们住在了一起，尝试过一些简单的活在当下的生活。即使只是一起吃早餐、聊天、做饭、看电影、躺在床上，都是美好的，我们像一对形影不离的双胞胎在共同编织着充满快乐和爱的生活。如果时间真的不再往前走，那多好。但时间仍然只会继续往前走。到了一定的年龄，似乎就要继续做对应该做的事情了。我们结婚了，但似乎不知道究竟结婚是什么。我们想尝试建立一个家庭，但似乎并不懂如何建立一个家庭。太多关系和任务围绕这我们，我们并不懂得如何处理，也许也不想去处理。某一天她跟我说，她看过外面的世界，那里有其他可能。我猜那是自由。我们卡在了某个地方，越想挣脱却束缚得越紧。


After graduating and going through the fear of finding a job and losing my job, life finally starts to settle down and I gradually start to accept and get used to living a routine life like everyone else. We live together and try to live a simple life of living in the moment. Even just eating breakfast, talking to each other, cooking, watching movies, and lying in bed together, is wonderful. We are like twins inseparable weaving a life full of joy and love together. If time does not go forward, it will be great. But time will only continue to move forward. At a certain age, it seems like it's time to move on to the right things to do. We get married, but don't seem to know exactly what it is to get married. We want to try to start a family, but don't seem to know how to start one. There are too many relationships and tasks surrounding us that we don't know how to handle and maybe don't want to handle. One day she tells me that she has seen the outside world and that there are other possibilities there. I guess that is freedom. We are stuck somewhere, and the more we try to break free the tighter the bondage becomes.
