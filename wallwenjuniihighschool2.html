生活继续以没有归属感的但要完成高考任务的状态继续……直到一位女孩出现了。我们的距离越来越近，我感觉到生活中又再次充满了笑容、笑声及爱。我们会经常相约到附近大学的校园里见面。坐在长椅上，有时我会不自觉地睡到她的大腿上，感觉整个世界也就剩我们两人了，时间也仿佛停止了。如果时间真的不再往前走，那多好。



Life continues with no sense of belonging but with the task of completing the National College Entrance Examination... until a girl comes along. We get closer and closer and I feel that life is once again filled with smiles, laughter and love. We often meet up on the campus of a nearby university. Sitting on a bench, sometimes I will unconsciously sleep on her lap, feeling that the whole world is left to the two of us, and time seems to have stopped. If time really does not go forward, it will be great.
