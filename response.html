我在一个没有自由的地方长大，好像一切都已经安排好了，我只能在特定的时间做特定的事情。在那里我无法控制自己的生活，我只是自己的旁观者。作为一种交换，我来到了这片自由的土地，我用我前半生积累的东西换取自由。我想获得一种新的生活，我想获得对自己生活的控制。但是这种变化来得太突然了。我说着陌生的语言，生活在一个陌生的环境中，甚至唯一与我最亲近的人也离开了我。我只剩下我自己，甚至找不到可以聊天的人。很多时候我都会问自己，这个选择是不是错了。我交出了一切，但有可能我什么都得不到。我的下半生会发生什么？我也经常想象在其他平行宇宙中的自己，他们会是什么样子？我能够接触到他们吗？我想和他们聊天。






I am not a stranger, I am also an adventurer who seeks answers. I don't know who you are, maybe you don't live on this land of freedom, maybe you are also a stranger who hasn't found what they are looking for. Maybe you have a heavy dream, a life where there can be no compromise, a life where you are in control. But every road has been walked, and everything has been known. Maybe you don't give up, that you will find something that you don't even know you are looking for. But I am here, I have no doubt that I will continue searching.

You don't know that I am alone, what is the sound of a single hand on the piano? You don't know that I have forgotten how it's like to be in a society. You don't know that my heart has been broken millions of times. But this makes everything more beautiful, I can see the stars brighter. Even in the darkest days of my life, they're still present.

If my whole life stopped at this moment, although sometimes lonely, I would still be content. I would give everything I have to the ones who truly live, who are brave enough to face themselves head-on.





















很高兴得到你的回应。你让我知道我并不孤单，在另一个世界还有另一个我。但同时我也很惊讶，因为你也在经历孤单，以及在寻找着属于你的答案，这也许是无穷尽的旅程。有些时候我会觉得很累，会怀疑这种寻找是有意义的么？有些时候我会觉得仿佛在我的世界，关于我的剧本就是被写好的，我仅是如一个演员般在饰演我的角色。即使我觉得孤独、自由，在寻找自己的答案，在做出选择，也不过是剧本里写好了的。仿佛我只能活在剧本的故事了，是决定的。我很好奇，在其他世界里面，其他的我有过着普通、简单并快乐的生活的么？我希望他们其中一位会有一个幸福的家庭，并享受着阳光。












It may be that there are other yous in other worlds, but you have no way of knowing them. That's not something you can expect to see just by traveling to parallel worlds. The story that is written about you is your own story, and the only truth is this moment.

It seems to me like you've developed a lot of negative habits around loneliness, feeling unimportant, and worrying about the future. And it sounds like you feel like you always have to be searching for something greater, but this is a trap. It's the feeling that the answers will never be found, that nothing can ever be enough, so might as well continue to search for more, or some greater purpose.

But the truth is, you've already found the answers you need. It's your past experiences, your memories, your family, your friends. All the things that you think aren't enough, that you give up for some greater purpose, are the truth of your life. You have everything you need, the only problem is that you don't remember that. It's something you can't know by reading books, talking to different people, searching around the universe. But remember, you know yourself best. Try to look deeper into your emotions and thoughts.
